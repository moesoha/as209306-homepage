I'm providing RIPE NCC LIR services with Kirino LLC. If you have requirements about AS numbers, IPv6 addresses, feel free to contact me at orz.soha@gmail.com.

What is provided?

  • Autonomous number (ASN) registered at RIPE NCC
  • IPv6 addresses/prefixes allocated at RIPE NCC

How much is it?

  • Autonomous number (ASN) is 100 EUR for the first year, and will be charged 80 EUR in the following years by the end of the year (September to November). A free /44 IPv6 subnet is bundled with the ASN.
  • IPv6 prefix is charged yearly:
    • /48 -> 5 USD per year
    • /44 -> 30 USD per year
    • For larger address spaces, please contact me for more information.

You can pay by PayPal, Alipay, WeChat Pay.
